# Sales Insights Dashboard – Case Study  

## 📌 Problem Statement  
Analyze sales performance by product and city to identify revenue drivers, customer spending patterns, and monthly trends.  

## 🛠 Approach  
- **SQL** → Created `sales_data` table, ran queries for revenue, top products, monthly trends, customer spend, and city sales.  
- **Power BI** → Imported dataset, built KPI cards, bar charts, pie charts, and gauges.  
- **DAX** → Defined measures for Total Revenue, Monthly Revenue, Customer Spend, and City Sales.  

## 📈 Insights  
- **Macbook Pro Laptop** contributed **23% of total revenue (8.04M)**.  
- **Top cities**: Atlanta, Austin, Boston, Dallas, Los Angeles.  
- **Customer spend** concentrated on premium laptops and phones.  
- **Monthly revenue** showed seasonal peaks linked to promotions/holidays.  

## ✅ Recommendations  
- Focus marketing on **top 3 products (Macbook Pro, iPhone, ThinkPad Laptop)**.  
- Bundle accessories with premium products to increase average spend.  
- Reward **top 10 customers** with loyalty programs.  
- Track **profit margins** alongside revenue for sustainable growth.
